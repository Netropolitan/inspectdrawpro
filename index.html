FAQ

Common questions.

What is InspectDraw Pro?

InspectDraw Pro is a field inspection app for Android tablets and Windows PCs. It turns your existing technical drawings into a searchable inspection canvas: find any labelled item on the plan, drop a marker, record its condition with notes and photos, and export a signed PDF report before you leave site.

Does InspectDraw Pro work offline?

Yes, fully. It was built for basements, plant rooms and older buildings with no signal. Everything is stored locally in a portable save file, so the workflow never stalls waiting for a connection.

What safety and compliance items can I track?

Anything that lives on a drawing: emergency lighting, exits and signage, fire alarm call points, fire doors and dampers, electrical distribution boards and isolation points, intruder alarms, ventilation, gas services, lightning protection, asbestos, fire containment walls, and water outlets and deadlegs for legionella monitoring. There are 43 premade marker types, plus custom markers for anything else.

My site drawings are out of date. Can I still use it?

Yes, and it will help you fix them. Your site team can walk the building in survey mode, pin what is actually there, photograph it and produce a report that brings your base drawings up to date. If your plans are paper-only or unreadable, our drawing and digitisation service redraws them into clean, searchable PDFs.

How does it help with alarm callouts?

When the fire or intruder panel reports a device number at 2am, type that number into the search and jump straight to its location on the drawing. No guesswork, no walking the building, and no calling out a contractor to find a spider on a PIR.

Can external compliance companies use it on sites they have never visited?

Yes. Arrive with the drawings already loaded and find every relevant location without hand-drawn maps or verbal directions. Run a full duration emergency light test with every circuit switch and device identified on your tablet, sign it all off in the app, and hand over a complete report at the end.

What devices does it run on?

Android tablets and Windows PCs. The same save file opens and edits identically on both, so findings recorded on site transfer straight to the desk.

How does InspectDraw Pro support compliance?

Compliance requires information that is current, accurate and relevant. Every inspection record is date-stamped, pinned to its exact location on the drawing and backed by photographs, so your records show the task was actually done rather than a ticked box.

How much does InspectDraw Pro cost?

The Enterprise / Estate licence is £25,000 one-off (ex VAT) and covers unlimited named users on one estate, deployment, a day of on-site training, and a 2-year warranty covering updates, support and fixes. Tailored services such as drawing digitisation, on-site survey and custom integrations are scoped and priced per engagement.

Can it be customised for our site or sector?

Yes. We built InspectDraw Pro ourselves, so we can adapt it: custom marker libraries, branded reports, bespoke survey checklists and integrations with the systems you already run. If you have an idea that would make it better, we will listen and build it.
