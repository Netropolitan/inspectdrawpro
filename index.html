The problem

Field inspections still run on paper, photos, and email chains.

Engineers print the drawing, scribble on it, take phone photos, and rebuild the report back at the desk. Findings get lost, photos detach from their location, and reports take days.

The solution

One app. Your drawing. A finished report.

InspectDraw Pro turns any technical PDF into a live inspection canvas. Search the plan, drop labelled markers, attach status, notes and photos, and export a signed report.

How it works

Three steps, on-site.

01

Find the location

Type a label, room number or circuit ID. Hits jump straight to the spot on the plan.

02

Mark & capture

Drop a premade marker or draw a shape. Set status, add notes, attach up to five photos.

03

Sign off & deliver

Sign on screen and hit Export. A clean PDF lists every finding, its status and its photos.

Core features

Built for the way engineers actually work.

Smart PDF text search

Custom shapes & colours

Inspection lists

Up to five photos per item, embedded into the save file.

Survey mode

Reusable templates

Portable save files

The origin

Not a marketing-led product.

Gary Brett

Concept Originator

Site Maintenance · 20+ years in the field

It was designed by someone who spent two decades doing exactly the job it now supports, building the tool he wished he'd had on every site walk.

Twenty years maintaining school buildings: PFI handovers, snagging and compliance, often run from afar with missing documentation and critical knowledge lost at every handover. He lived the pain of paper drawings, outdated floor plans and a phone full of photos.

After retiring, he conceived InspectDraw Pro. His sons built it.
