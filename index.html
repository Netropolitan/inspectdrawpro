The problem

Replace the field-tool pile.

Printed drawings, scribbled notes, a camera roll full of photos, and a report rebuilt back at the desk. Findings get lost, photos detach from their location, and reports take days. InspectDraw Pro replaces the whole pile with one direct workflow.

The solution

One app. Your drawing. A finished report.

InspectDraw Pro turns any technical PDF into a live inspection canvas. Search the plan, drop labelled markers, attach status, notes and photos, and export a signed report.

The origin

Not a marketing-led product.

Gary Brett

Concept Originator

Site Maintenance · 20+ years in the field

It was designed by someone who spent two decades doing exactly the job it now supports, building the tool he wished he'd had on every site walk.

Twenty years maintaining school buildings: PFI handovers, snagging and compliance, often run from afar with missing documentation and critical knowledge lost at every handover. Gary lived the pain of paper drawings, outdated floor plans and a phone full of photos, then the hours spent rebuilding reports back at the desk.

After retirement, he conceived InspectDraw Pro. His sons, who had been working in industrial XR solutions for five years at Beyond XR, built it.
